Welcome to Mandy Godding's Theatre Arts

We are a long established dedicated Theatre Arts School located in the centre of Gloucester. With over 25 years experience, we offer a comprehensive education in the performing arts.

At Mandy Godding's Theatre Arts you can choose to specialise in dance, singing or acting, but we pride ourselves on addressing all performance disciplines with equal priority.

Highly experienced teachers with professional experience work with students to give them the tools to take their skills to the next level as well as build confidence and assertion. Our friendly, approachable staff encourage every child to reach their full potential.

Classes are available in ballet, tap, modern, jazz, singing and drama, as well as providing the opportunity to work on productions, which hold the same demands as professional theatre.

We provide training for everyone from the smallest child needing a safe friendly environment to learn, to those wishing to follow a career in the performing arts.

If you would like to find out whether we can accommodate for your individual needs, please visit our contact page.

Why Choose Mandy Godding's Theatre Arts?

Mandy Godding's Theatre Arts is the most comprehensive theatre arts school in Gloucestershire. With our links to theatrical institutions and the West End, Mandy Godding's TA is one of the best places to start your education and progress into professional theatre. The most nervous pupils are encouraged to find the confidence to perform and have fun!

With studios in the centre of Gloucester and branches in Churchdown and Abbeydale, we are easily accessible.
